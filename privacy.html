Privacy Policy for Sanatan App

Effective Date: July 23, 2026

1. Introduction

Shree built the Sanatan app as a Free app. This SERVICE is provided by Shree at no cost and is intended for use as is. This page informs visitors regarding policies with the collection, use, and disclosure of Personal Information for anyone using the Service.

2. Information Collection and Use

For a better experience while using our Service, you may be required to provide certain personally identifiable information. The information requested is retained on your device or stored securely on our cloud infrastructure to manage your account.

To manage your secure login, this app uses Google Sign-In. By logging in, the app collects the following information provided by your Google account:

  • Email Address
  • First and Last Name
  • Profile Picture URL

This data is securely stored using Google's Firebase infrastructure and is only used to manage your profile, display your post credentials, and authenticate your access to the app.

3. Media & File Uploads

When you share photos or videos in the app, these files are uploaded to and processed by our cloud media partner (Cloudinary) to compress and deliver optimized media content across the network efficiently.

4. Third-Party Services

The app uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. Below are links to the privacy policies of the third-party service providers used by the app:

  • Google Play Services
  • AdMob (Google Ads)
  • Firebase Authentication & Firestore
  • Cloudinary (Media Hosting)

5. Data Deletion Policy

You have the right to request the deletion of your personal data. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data from our database, please send an email to sanatan.support@gmail.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request" from the email address associated with your account. Your account and media data will be permanently deleted within 7 days.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Any updates will be notified by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
